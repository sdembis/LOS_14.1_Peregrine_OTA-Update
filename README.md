# LOS_14.1_Peregrine_OTA-update

Go to 'Settings' > scroll down to 'About phone' > at top of screen click 'LineageOS updates', click the refresh button at top right of screen, choose the latest update and when the download completes, click install. Your device will reboot to recovery and install the update, then reboot to the updated installation.

Reference:  https://wiki.lineageos.org/devices/peregrine/update
